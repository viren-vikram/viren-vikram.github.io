Retail Customer Insights using IRI data

IRI data is useful in understanding retail customer behaviour in long term as well as short term. Multiple experiments on such longitudinal data of customers, retail stores, markets and various promotion was utilized to find insights. Such insights are leveraged to manage market promotions, Brand switching, customer's centric approaches in cost effective way. Techniques used are as below
  • RFM
  • Ordered Logit Model
  • Effects of promotion sensitivity of different markets & brands using random effects regression model ( Panel Regression)
  • Brnad Switching
